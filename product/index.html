Product

One platform. Every workflow.

CSorro OS brings your dashboard, projects, people, storage, studio review, CRM, reports, invoices and AI into one clean workspace.

Dashboard

Start every day with one view of what matters: active projects, reviews, tasks, storage, activity, clients and upcoming work. No messy mission control. Just a clean dashboard your team understands instantly.

Projects

Run project work with tasks, comments, files, approvals, whiteboards, activity and timelines connected together.

People

Invite employees, freelancers, clients and guests. Assign roles, control access, and override permissions per person where needed.

Workspace Storage

Storage belongs to the workspace, not random pages. Organise files into libraries, link them to projects, version them, comment on them and control access.

Studio Review

Upload clips, images and drafts. Leave timestamped feedback, request changes, approve versions and keep the review attached to the project.

Reports & Invoices

Track completed work, activity, time, client updates and project delivery. Build the foundation for reports, invoices and business admin from inside the same OS.

CORE AI

CORE will understand the context of your workspace: projects, people, storage, messages, reviews, clients and reports — so you can ask what needs attention and act without searching through five tools.

Private by default

Workspaces, projects, files and rooms are hidden unless the owner grants access. Public showcase is always opt-in.
