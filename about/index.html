About CSorro OS

One operating system for the work behind every great business.

CSorro OS is being built for teams who have outgrown scattered apps, buried messages and disconnected workflows. It brings projects, people, assets, clients, approvals, reports, invoices and AI into one clean workspace.

Explore the product →Meet the founder
The idea

Work should move from brief to delivery without losing context.

CSorro connects the creative side of delivery with the business side of operations — so the team, the client, the files and the decisions all stay together.

Why it exists

Most teams are not short on effort. They are short on a system.

Modern work is split between chat, drives, task boards, review tools, spreadsheets, CRMs and invoice systems. Every handoff creates friction. Every missing file costs time. Every buried decision slows the next step. CSorro OS exists to make the whole operation visible, connected and easier to run.
